
-The prefab will only be visible on canvas.

-Recommended canvas mode - Scale With Screen Size (no Constant Pixel Size)

-If you want to make a new cube, copy the prefab, copy and rename the animations,
(replace the animations inside the animator and drag the new animator to Dice) 
you can change the copied animations and also change the color of the cube 
in the graphic editor, replace the cube sprites in the animation.
